https://www.bbc.com/ is appealing because :

  1. It is easy to navigate.
  2. It provides news that is less biased than other platforms, like social media.
  3. It doesn't keep track of every news article you see and makes the algorithm make you see more of the same type of article

I encourage people to visit the site if they want information and want to see a easy to navigate website

https://www.tiktok.com/foryou is unappealing to me because:

  1. The site is meant to be viewed in a mobile app, so the website takes up only the center of the screen.
  2. It can be hard to navigate the cite due to almost everything on the screen being a button.
  3. The algorithm can put videos you don't want to see on your screen due to you watching a video that was slightly similar.

I do not encourage anyone to view this site on a PC. Stick to the app version.

The site could be improved by making the videos bigger and putting more space between the buttons.
